FEATURED NEWS

On March 10th, 2017 Gamma Theta Chapter showcased 9 neophytes. This historic event marks the largest line our chapter has crossed to date. Special shout out to our Brothers from Beta Sigma Chapter, Gamma Upsilon Chapter, and UW-Milwaukee Colony for their support.

Founding Brother Richard Montez is bringing about changes to his community in San Antonio by running to be City Councilman of District 5. We wish him the best of luck on the campaign trail.

Please join the First Nationally Recognized Latino Fraternity as we proudly present our newest member(s) to the University of Wisconsin-Madison community. Presentation will be at Bascom Hill on March 10th at 8:15PM.

We would like to thank all of the support we received during our first Spanish Language Book Drive. Thanks to y'all we were able to collect 115 books!

By telling our stories, we seek to build knowledge, reflect on and show the broader community the ways we show up for one another, build safety for ourselves and love our communities.

Requirements

Lambda Theta Phi was born to promote the spirit of brotherhood, to protect the rights of Latino students in his/her quest for an education, to promote the rich Latino culture, and to maximize leadership potentials to provide guidance to our community. Membership is open to all males, regardless of, race culture, or creed. Lambda Theta Phi looks for men of vision, who can assist in our mission to accomplish the goals set forth by our founders.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

What is an Interest Group?

Interest Groups of Lambda Theta Phi are organized as part of the preliminary phase to the induction process. We Do Not Rush. We want to make sure that potential future brothers of our fraternity get more familiar with the greek system as well as our fraternity and its history. Also the group of interested men will learn to bond, learn to work as an organized group, learn more about the Fraternity, and get to know the current brothers.
